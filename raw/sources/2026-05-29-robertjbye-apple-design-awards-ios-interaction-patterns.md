## Linked primary source: Apple Design Awards 2026 finalists

Title: "Apple Design Awards - 2026 finalists - Apple Developer"

Apple describes the awards as recognizing innovation, ingenuity, and technical achievement in app and game design. The 2026 finalist categories are:

- Delight and Fun
- Inclusivity
- Innovation
- Interaction
- Social Impact
- Visuals and Graphics

### Finalists and notable Apple-described mechanisms

Delight and Fun:

- Blippo+ — retro-futurist synchronized broadcast experience; detailed world-building, ancient graphics/fonts/buttons.
- Metaballs — iOS/visionOS spatial blobs with poking/pulling interactions and USDZ export.
- grug — simple iOS daily wisdom app; hand-drawn status bar; no login/cloud/extraneous features.
- PowerWash Simulator — haptic feedback per hose nozzle; broad Apple platform support.
- Is This Seat Taken? — scene objects produce playful responses; UI hides Easter eggs; no timers.
- Ball x Pit — ricochet combat with retro visuals and HDR displays.

Inclusivity:

- Guitar Wiz — SwiftUI toolkit with VoiceOver, Dynamic Type, Increased Contrast, Differentiate Without Color.
- Hearing Buddy — real-time captions/summaries using Foundation Models and on-device speech-to-text.
- Structured — SwiftUI planner; downtime-first scanning; Foundation Models suggest/pre-fill tasks.
- Sago Mini Jinja's Garden — natural swipe-to-move controls and no-reading interactions for ages 3–6.
- Pine Hearts — settings for text legibility, custom controls, motion/sensory easing.
- Civilization VII — representation of diverse leaders/civilizations.

Innovation:

- Detail: AI Video Editor — identifies magic moments, removes silence, auto-edits single shot from multiple angles, Foundation Models teleprompter script.
- NBA: Live Games & Scores — visionOS multi-game viewing, wall-size stats, Apple Immersive Video, Spatial Audio, 3D scoreboard/player rosters.
- D-Day: The Camera Soldier — visionOS immersive video, Spatial Audio, interactive activities for historical storytelling.
- TR-49 — iOS/iPadOS nonlinear narrative and puzzles; built with ink scripting language.
- Blue Prince — player builds mansion rooms while uncovering hidden narrative.
- Pickle Pro — RealityKit visionOS game with mixed-reality blending and SharePlay personas.

Interaction:

- The Outsiders: Athlete Tracker — Training Readiness Score visualization combining load, sleep, resting heart rate; workout goals across workout types.
- Moonlitt: Moon Phase Tracker — SwiftUI utility, Liquid Glass integration, easy onboarding, broad platform support.
- Tide Guide: Charts & Tables — Liquid Glass, custom animations, full-screen weather/tide charts, widgets, sky-matching palette.
- TR-49 — audio design where narrator reacts to every discovery.
- Sago Mini Jinja's Garden — no-reading interactions and effortless swipe-to-move controls.
- Grand Mountain Adventure 2 — touch controls mapped naturally to skiing with smooth responsive gestures.

Social Impact:

- Primary: News in Depth — spatial news app with minimal UI, editorial team, anti-clickbait framing.
- Katha Room — Indian folklore bedtime stories, Gond-art-inspired UI, sound design.
- Harvee — Apple Watch recovery guidance using HRV/resting heart/sleep/activity; on-device foundation models.
- Consume Me — game mechanics mirror eating-disorder narrative themes.
- Despelote — autobiographical/cultural preservation via movement/eavesdropping-driven narrative.
- Spilled! — cozy cleanup game about restoring polluted waterways, by a solo developer living on a houseboat.

Visuals and Graphics:

- Tide Guide — charts, custom animations, sky-matching palette.
- Caradise — visionOS auto museum with detailed rendering and Spatial Audio.
- (Not Boring) Camera — tactile retro camera UI with giant buttons, haptic scroll wheels, SuperRAW, professional filters.
- Cyberpunk 2077 Ultimate Edition — Apple silicon, Metal shaders, MetalFX frame interpolation/denoising, path tracing, For This Mac setting.
- Arknights: Endfield — iPhone/iPad large-scale 3D RPG, hardware-accelerated ray tracing, Spatial Audio.
- SILT — monochrome light/shadow, possession mechanic, camera scaling, environmental theme.
