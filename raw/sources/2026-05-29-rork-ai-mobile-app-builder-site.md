```text
How does Rork work?
Describe your app idea in plain English and Rork generates a real, working mobile app. Rork offers two modes: Rork Pro builds cross-platform apps with React Native and Expo, while Rork Max builds native iOS apps with SwiftUI. You can test your app instantly on your phone or in your browser.
Does Rork build real mobile apps?
Yes. Rork builds real, native mobile apps, not web wrappers or prototypes. Rork Pro uses React Native and Expo, the same production framework behind Discord, Shopify, Coinbase, and nearly 30% of top 100 apps on the App Store. Rork Max builds native SwiftUI apps compiled with Xcode, the exact same toolchain every iOS developer uses. Both produce apps you can publish to the App Store.
What is the difference between Rork Pro and Rork Max?
Rork Pro ($25-$100/month) builds cross-platform apps with React Native and Expo that run on iOS, Android, and web. It is great for building apps quickly across all platforms. Rork Max ($200+/month) builds native SwiftUI apps for Apple platforms including iPhone, iPad, Apple Watch, and Vision Pro. Max produces premium Apple-native UI, is powered by Opus 4.8 for higher quality code and design, and unlocks advanced capabilities like widgets, Live Activities, Dynamic Island, Siri intents, ARKit, HealthKit, 3D games with SceneKit, and more. Max also includes one-click iPhone install, two-click App Store publishing, and priority chat support. Pro subscribers get 5 free Max uses per week to try it out.
```
