# Molly Studio

## Identity
Molly Studio was a design/product studio acquired by [[Shopify]] to form a Product Design Studio focused on AI-native commerce. Its captured thesis pages include [[Molly Studio AI-native Interface Thesis Collection]], [[Context Transfer Protocol (CTP)]], [[Molly Studio OpenAI Endgame]], and [[Molly Studio API-ification of Everything]], which collectively supply practitioner vocabulary for [[AI-native Commerce Interfaces]], [[Intent-based Computing]], [[API-ification of Services]], [[MCP Tool Connectors]], [[Adaptive UI Generation]], context portability, and app frontends becoming less central.

## Evidence
- [[Molly Studio AI-native Interface Thesis Collection]] records the homepage/acquisition page, the Shopify Product Design Studio context, and the collection-level AI-native interface thesis map.

- [[Molly Studio OpenAI Endgame]] records Molly's thesis that OpenAI may collapse apps, OS, identity, communication, and generated UI into one AI-mediated platform; Tolaria treats this as concept evidence, not roadmap proof.
