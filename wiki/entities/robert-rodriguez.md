# Robert Rodriguez

## Identity
Robert Rodriguez is the filmmaker used in [[How to walk through walls]] as the central case study for [[Hacker Mindset]]: he made and sold *El Mariachi* by acting on the practical substrate of filmmaking rather than on film-school or Hollywood process conventions.

## Aliases
- Robert Rodriguez
- Rodriguez

## Key Attributes
| Attribute | Value |
|---|---|
| Case in current source | Low-budget feature-film production and career entry |
| Mechanism illustrated | Collapsing role, crew, lighting, shot-coverage, editing, and budget assumptions into hands-on technical control. |
| Evidence caveat | The article relies on Rodriguez's published diary/anecdotes and Karlsson's interpretation; it is a qualitative case, not an empirical proof. |

## Evidence
- [[How to walk through walls]] says Rodriguez made *El Mariachi* for about $7,000, did much of the technical work himself, minimized takes/film stock by editing in his head, and treated "movie experience" as less useful than direct experience making and manipulating images.
