# LightRAG

## Identity
LightRAG is a retrieval-augmented generation framework referenced in Tolaria as a comparator in Cognee's vendor-published memory/retrieval evaluation material. The current Tolaria evidence does not independently evaluate LightRAG; it preserves LightRAG mainly to make the Cognee benchmark comparison and contradiction traceable through [[Cognee AI Memory Platform, Evals, and Case-study Claims]].

## Aliases
- LightRAG

## Key Attributes
- Appears as a comparator against Cognee, Mem0, and Graphiti in the captured Cognee evaluation material.
- Is relevant to [[Graph-aware Retrieval Evals]] and [[Grounded RAG Reliability Pattern]] as an example of retrieval/memory benchmark comparison vocabulary.
- Evidence grade in Tolaria is limited to its role in the Cognee source; no standalone LightRAG primary-source ingest has been completed here.

## Evidence
- [[Cognee AI Memory Platform, Evals, and Case-study Claims]] notes that Cognee's research page claims the most contextually accurate and human-like answers, while the public comparison chart showed LightRAG above Cognee on Human-like Correctness.
- [[Cognee Memory and Evaluation Claims Assessment]] preserves the same benchmark caveat: LightRAG is useful as a comparator, not as proof of a Tolaria/Hermes adoption target.
