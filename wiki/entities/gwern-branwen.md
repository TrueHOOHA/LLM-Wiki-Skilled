# Gwern Branwen

## Identity
Gwern Branwen is the author of [[On Seeing Through and Unseeing: The Hacker Mindset]], a practitioner essay that Tolaria uses for the reductionist hacker-mindset frame connecting abstraction leaks, security mindset, speedrunning, stage magic, weird machines, and lower-level substrate inspection.
