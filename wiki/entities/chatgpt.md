# ChatGPT

## Identity
ChatGPT is OpenAI's conversational LLM product. In this Tolaria source, it is the LLM assistance condition used for educational essay writing in [[Your Brain on ChatGPT: Accumulation of Cognitive Debt when Using an AI Assistant for Essay Writing Task]].

## Evidence
- [[Your Brain on ChatGPT: Accumulation of Cognitive Debt when Using an AI Assistant for Essay Writing Task]] reports lower EEG connectivity, lower essay ownership, and weaker quote recall for the LLM group relative to search/brain-only conditions, while explicitly warning against broad media overclaims.
