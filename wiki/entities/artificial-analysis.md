# Artificial Analysis

## Identity
Artificial Analysis is an AI model benchmarking and market-analysis site whose Intelligence Index is used in Tolaria as a comparative signal, not as a direct substitute for task-specific Hermes/Codex evaluations.

## Aliases
- Artificial Analysis
- Artificial Analysis Intelligence Index
- AA Intelligence Index
- Artificial Analysis Intelligence Index v4.0

## Key Attributes
- Relevant metric: Artificial Analysis Intelligence Index.
- Current methodology inspected for this source: v4.0.4, March 2026.
- v4.0 incorporates 10 evaluations: GDPval-AA, tau2-Bench Telecom, Terminal-Bench Hard, SciCode, AA-LCR, AA-Omniscience, IFBench, Humanity's Last Exam, GPQA Diamond, and CritPt.
- The methodology page says the index is useful for comparing models but has limitations and may not apply directly to every use case.
- Model pages inspected for the laptop-progress chart corroborate several charted scores, but not the full "smartest runnable on 128 GB MacBook Pro" selection rule.
