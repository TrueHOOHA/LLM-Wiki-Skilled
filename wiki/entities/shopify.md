# Shopify

## Identity
Shopify is a commerce platform referenced in [[Molly Studio AI-native Interface Thesis Collection]] as the acquirer of [[Molly Studio]] and the organizational home for a Product Design Studio focused on AI-native commerce. In this Tolaria source, Shopify's relevance is strategic: Molly argues that Shopify's SKU repository and commerce infrastructure may become more valuable if commerce actions move into AI interactions and [[Post-app Interfaces]].
